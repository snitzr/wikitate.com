What is this?

This website helps users find and create transcripts to YouTube videos.

How to use

Copy and search by YT link or install browser plugin.

Uses

  • Access for the deaf and hard of hearing. Greater than 5% of the total world population has significant hearing loss (source).
  • Make video more searchable. Search engines (like Google) work best with text.
  • Open up a video to more languages. Translate a video into another language.
  • Browse without sound. Forgot your headphones?
