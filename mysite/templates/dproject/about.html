Purpose

  • Wikitate helps you add transcripts to other people's YouTube videos.
  • Access for the deaf and hard of hearing. Greater than 5% of the total world population has significant hearing loss.
  • Make video more discoverable. Search engines (like Google) work best with text.
  • Open a video to a larger audience by translating a video into another language.
  • Browse without sound.

Navigation

Copy and paste a YouTube link into the search box.

Access Wikitate directly from YouTube with the view in Wikitate bookmarklet. Add this link to your bookmarks and use it while in YouTube to instantly jump the video into the Wikitate player: view in Wikitate.

Next Steps

  • Native iPhone app. Safari on iPhone limits some non-native video playback control and this necessitates the need for a native app.
  • Paid transcription and translation requests.
  • Crowdsourced transcriptions and translations.
  • Improve usability and accessibility.
