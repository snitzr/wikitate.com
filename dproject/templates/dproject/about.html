What is this?

This website helps users find and create transcripts to YouTube videos.

How to use

Copy and search by YT link or install browser plugin.

Uses

  • Access for the deaf and hard of hearing. Greater than 10% of the total U.S. population is deaf or hard of hearing (source).
  • Make video more searchable.
  • Open up a video to more languages.
  • Browse without sound.
